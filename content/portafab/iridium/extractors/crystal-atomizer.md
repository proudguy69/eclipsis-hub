The Crystal Atomizer is an Extractor that is placed on an Iridium Crystal. Its production varies depending on the size of the crystal in the Atomizer. Once built on the crystal, the Atomizer will extract iridium from it and expel it from its output pipe. The Atomizer produces 10 iridium/s from a maximum sized crystal, and does not deplete the crystal. If the internal storage of the Crystal Atomizer is above 90% storage (45 iridium), it will fill the crystal it is attached to at a rate of 2 iridium per second. This does not expend any iridium.
