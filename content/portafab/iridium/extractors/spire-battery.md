## Description

The Spire Battery is an extractor structure in Eclipsis that produces iridium at a rate of 30 iridium per second (i/s) and can store up to 650 iridium. It is placed on Spire Pipes, which are found within spires or beneath the ground. The Spire Battery is particularly useful for Sprawl Bases and Ruin Bases due to its high production rate and storage capacity.

One of the key advantages of the Spire Battery is its automatic protection and concealment. Since Spire Pipes only spawn in specific locations like spires and underground structure blocks, the Spire Battery is inherently protected from easy access by enemies. This makes it a reliable source of iridium for rushes and late-game strategies.

However, the Spire Battery has some limitations. It can only be placed on Spire Pipes, which are less common than Well Pipes. This rarity can make it challenging to find suitable locations for the Spire Battery, especially in the early stages of the game. Additionally, if not properly protected, the Spire Battery can be easily destroyed by enemy attacks.

## Strategies

- **Sprawl and Ruin Bases**: Utilize the Spire Battery in Sprawl Bases and Ruin Bases to take advantage of its high production rate and storage capacity.
- **Concealment**: Leverage the natural protection offered by spires and underground structure blocks to keep your Spire Battery safe from enemy attacks.
- **Rush Strategies**: Use the Spire Battery as an easy iridium source for rushes, allowing you to quickly gather resources for aggressive plays.
