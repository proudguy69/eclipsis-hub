The Well Pump is an Extractor. It must be placed on a Well Pipe and once built, will pump iridium from the Iridium Ocean to its output pipe at a rate of 10 iridium per second. If the Pressurizer is attached to spots on a Well Pump's pump cluster, its production is increased by 10.
