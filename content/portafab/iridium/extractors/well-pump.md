## Description

The Well Pump is an essential Extractor in Eclipsis, designed to extract iridium from the Iridium Ocean. It must be placed on a Well Pipe and will pump iridium to its output pipe at a rate of 10 iridium per second. The production can be further increased by attaching Pressurizers to spots on a Well Pump's pump cluster, boosting production by an additional 10 iridium per second per pump.

Well Pumps are highly efficient for iridium production throughout all stages of gameplay. They are particularly valuable in Sprawl Bases, which rely heavily on multiple Well Pumps for their iridium supply. The Well Pump can be upgraded twice using a Portafab, adding functionalities such as a built-in Fabricator and a built-in Distributor.

## Strategies

- **Early Game Efficiency**: Use Well Pumps early in the game to establish a steady iridium income.
- **Sprawl Bases**: Build multiple Well Pumps in a Sprawl Base configuration to maximize iridium extraction.
- **Upgrades**: Upgrade Well Pumps to add extra functionalities like built-in Fabricators and Distributors.
- **Pressurizer Integration**: Attach Pressurizers to boost production rates significantly.
